DINING

The Ultimate Dining Destination In The South

Featuring an exquisite selection of six dining spaces atop our resident coastal hillock, Jetwing Lighthouse is perhaps one of the finest dining destinations to exist on the sunny southern coastline of our tropical isle. In addition, you may indulge in the luxury of choosing between five more Signature Dining experiences as a pleasure of our legendary Sri Lankan hospitality.

For fine dining, The Cinnamon Room at Jetwing Lighthouse is a magnificent dinner-only restaurant that specialises in a number of seafood delicacies, while the Cardamom Café is a standout among Galle restaurants – operating for 24 hours a day, with a constant flow of food and drink through generous buffets or delicious snacks and refreshments. Our world-renowned executive chef, Nihal Senanayake, also brings his culinary prowess directly to your table at Nihal’s, where each dish has been carefully studied to the extent of its molecular structure to serve you exquisite global cuisine with a signature local touch. Finally, for a taste of Italy on Sri Lankan shores, Jetwing Lighthouse also houses Lorenzo’s Pizza Bar with its wide selection of meat, seafood, vegan, vegetarian and even kids’ pizzas to cater to every palate.

To wash down your meals in style, Jetwing Lighthouse features a sophisticated watering hole with an extensive range of cocktails, mocktails and everything in between – the Coats of Arms Bar, which is a nostalgic, old-world space with rustic charms to keep you in high spirits till midnight every day.

Finally, as a speciality of our hospitality, Jetwing Lighthouse offers five distinct Signature Dining experiences if you wish to indulge in your meals with a more personalised touch at a setting of your choosing. Whether you choose to enjoy a BBQ on our rocky shores, treat your loved one to a romantic meal in our gazebo, wake up to a champagne breakfast overlooking the ocean, escape to the privacy of our romantic spa or even to the comforts of your own room, our home of Sri Lankan hospitality looks forward to crafting a truly unique dining experience for you.

THE CINNAMON ROOM

Fine Dining Atop A Southern Coastal Hillock

Inspired by the exotic spice that drew travellers to our shores for centuries, The Cinnamon Room at Jetwing Lighthouse embodies this historical luxury in one of the most enchanting locations for fine dining experiences in Sri Lanka. Lavishly spread across a spacious room, adjoining a terrace on the second floor of our home of Sri Lankan hospitality, this fine dining restaurant is open only for dinner but features an exquisite à la carte menu with a number of daily specials and other culinary delights. With a selection of signature seafood delicacies caught from the depths of our southern seas, to fresh produce handpicked from our local markets, the Cinnamon Room is a must-visit fine dining restaurant on your holiday in Galle.
Cinnamon Room Menu

Cardamom Café

24-Hour Dining In Galle

Open to treat your taste buds at any hour of the day or night, the Cardamom Café at Jetwing Lighthouse is always ready to serve you. Perhaps one of the only Galle restaurants that offer 24 hour dining, the Cardamom Café is a welcome eatery known for its generous buffet spreads & set menus filled with flavours from around the world. You may choose to dine within air-conditioned comforts indoors, or opt to enjoy your meals amidst the ocean breeze outdoors. Regardless of where you pull up a seat, you will still enjoy the luxury of choosing from extensive menus that include main courses, light refreshments, kids’ meals, and even specialised wholesome vegan and vegetarian delights with our exclusive Heart Healthy Menu.
Cardamom Café Menu

Nihal's

With Compliments From The Chef

Named after our world-renowned executive chef Nihal Senanyake, Nihal’s offers a quintessential dining experience. Specialising in a range of global culinary delights with a signature Sri Lankan touch, executive chef Nihal adopts a novel approach to the meals on the menu here. By studying culinary influences from around the world and using the molecular structure of flavours to create new dishes, your time at Nihal’s is bound to be one of the most exciting dining experiences in the city of Galle. And with the added pleasures of a daily happy hour, signature dishes, and specially curated menus, your taste buds will always have something to come back for.
Nihal's Menu

Signature Dining

Special Settings Upon Our Coastal Hillock

Lavishly complementing the luxurious accommodation at Jetwing Lighthouse, our home of Sri Lankan hospitality offers the indulgence of four unique Signature Dining options for memorable meals by the southern seas. A specialty of our hospitality across the island, each of the six is designed to be an enchanting sensory experience with mouthwatering meals set against stunning backdrops. Be it as a private BBQ for two among our coastal rocks, or as a breakfast overlooking the ocean, and even as a romantic dining experience in the spa, or simply from the comforts of your own room.
